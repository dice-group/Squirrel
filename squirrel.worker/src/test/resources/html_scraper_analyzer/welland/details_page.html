Civic Addresses

Abstract This spatial data set contains civic address points for the City of Welland. The addresses are maintained internally by the Planning department. New addresses are added as they are created. Existing addresses are ammended when necessary, or when errors are encountered. There may be more than one address point contained within a parcel. Address points are generally positioned on the centre of the building or unit that it applies to.
