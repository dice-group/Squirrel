    Rockville Forest Conservation Easements (from Rockville)
    Shared by RockvilleMD on September 13, 2013 . Updated a month ago .
    This service contains the forest conservation easements within the City of Rockville and provides supplemental data such as the approved Forest Conservation Plan number, Montgomery County Land Records reference number (if available), size and type of easement, and notes on forest conservation credits. There are two types of forest conservation easements: one that protects forests and one that protects individual trees. Easements protecting forests are placed over the forested areas only within the property boundary and are designated as “partial”. Easements protecting individual trees are usually placed over the entire property and refer back to the approved Forest Conservation Plan to show which trees are covered by the easement. These easements are designated as “entire.” Street trees are protected under the Maryland Roadside Tree Law and therefore, not subject to forest conservation easements.
